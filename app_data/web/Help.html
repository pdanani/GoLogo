I need help using goLogoLo! What can I do?

Step 1: Open a new project!

First create a new project from the welcome dialog or from the top toolbar.

Step 2: Add Items to Your New Canvas

On the right pane (the edit components pane), add items to your canvas by selecting from a number of options such as: Add text, Add Image, etc.

Step 3: Edit Canvas Items

Select your items in the canvas and use the Edit Components Pane to edit your layers.

Step 4: Remove Canvas Items

Should you wish to remove a layer, select it from the canvas and then click on the "Delete Selected Component" button.

Step 5: Move Items and Resize them

By clicking and dragging on a component you can move selected items in the canvas up, down and around the canvas, which could represent the "order" of the canvas components.

Step 6: Save the To Do List

Once you have completed your work or would like to take a break from creating your masterpiece, you can press the save button and give your file a name, then save your file and open it again later.

Step 7: Export your work to an Image File!

Press the Export button when you wish to export your goLogoLo canvas into an image file that you can use for your company, website or other business adventures!
